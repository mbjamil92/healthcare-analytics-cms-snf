# Skilled Nursing Facility QRP outputs

The processed files in this folder summarize Ballad counties' SNF Quality Reporting Program data across the 2022–2025 snapshots (CSS assets in `archive/` are irrelevant for analysis).

## Files
- `snf_trend_summary.csv`: Year-over-year average and median scores per measure.
- `snf_multi_year_quality.csv`: Facility-level snapshots with measure values and contact details.

## Quick trends (2022–2025)
- **Discharge to Community Rate** dipped from a 52.41% average (2022) to 48.68% (2024) with a 2025 recovery to 50.93%; 2025 median 53.71% outpaces the average.
- **Preventable Readmission Rate** climbed from 7.86% (2022) to 10.77% (2025), indicating higher returns to acute care.
- **Medication Review Rate** rose steadily (93.38% → 97.40%), suggesting strong adherence to medication reconciliation practices.
- **Falls with Major Injury** improved (average 1.27% → 1.08%) alongside lower medians (1.00% → 0.94%).
- **Pressure Ulcer Rate** declined overall (3.46% → 2.62%) after a 2024 low of 2.56%.
- **Healthcare-Associated Infection Rate** trended downward (8.10 → 7.41), with medians falling from 8.05 to 7.06.
- **MSPB (Medicare Spending per Beneficiary)** eased slightly (1.20 → 1.16) with 2025 median matching the average.
- **Functional status baselines**: 2024 only—Mobility avg 44.64 (median 42.85), Self-Care avg 49.54 (median 50.75).

Use these points directly when responding to Khahn; refer to the CSVs for detailed facility-level context.
